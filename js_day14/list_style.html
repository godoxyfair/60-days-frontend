List of the tallest rockets ever launched.

  • Saturn V: 110.6 meters (363 feet)
  • N1: 105 meters (344 feet)
  • Ares I-X: 94.2 meters (309 feet)
  • Delta IV Heavy: 72 meters (236 feet)
  • Falcon Heavy: 70 meters (230 feet)
  • Angara-A5: 64 meters (210 feet)
  • Delta IV Medium: 62.50 meters (205.5 feet)
  • Ariane 4: 58.72 meters (192.65 feet)
  • Atlas V: 58.30 meters (191.27 feet)
  • Space Shuttle: 56.14 meters (184.2 feet)
